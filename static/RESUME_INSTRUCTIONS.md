# Resume Setup Instructions

## How to Add Your Resume

1. **Create your resume** using one of these tools:
   - **Canva** (easiest, has great templates): https://www.canva.com/resumes/templates/
   - **Google Docs** (simple and free)
   - **Microsoft Word**
   - **LaTeX** (for professional academic resumes)
   - **Figma** (for design-focused resumes)

2. **Export as PDF** - Make sure to save/export your resume as a PDF file

3. **Rename the file** to `resume.pdf`

4. **Place it in this folder** (`static/resume.pdf`)

5. **Test the download** - Visit your homepage and click "Download Resume"

## Resume Tips

### What to Include:
- **Contact Information**: Name, Email, Phone, Location, LinkedIn, GitHub
- **Professional Summary**: 2-3 sentences about your expertise
- **Skills**: Programming languages, frameworks, tools (match your portfolio)
- **Education**: Degree, University, Year, GPA (if good)
- **Experience**: Internships, projects, work experience
- **Projects**: Highlight 3-5 best projects from your portfolio
- **Achievements**: Awards, certifications, hackathons

### Design Tips:
- Keep it **1 page** (2 pages max if you have extensive experience)
- Use **consistent formatting** and fonts
- Include **action verbs**: Built, Developed, Implemented, Designed
- **Quantify achievements**: "Improved performance by 40%"
- Match the **color scheme** of your portfolio (cyan #9de1f5 and green #33c46b)
- Use **clean, modern design** - avoid overly decorative elements

### Recommended Template Style:
Since your portfolio uses a modern tech aesthetic with cyan and green gradients, consider:
- Clean, minimalist design
- Use your brand colors as accents
- Modern sans-serif fonts (like Space Grotesk to match your site)
- Clear section headers
- Plenty of white space
